About Us

Learn about our mission, vision, and the values that drive our work

Sakuntala Charitable Trust is a nationally recognized non-profit organization committed to serving society through a wide range of humanitarian initiatives across India. Established with a strong vision of upliftment and social justice, we work actively to create positive change.

Our mission is to create a more equitable and compassionate society by empowering the underprivileged, protecting the vulnerable, and promoting inclusive development.

Through educational programs, legal aid, health initiatives, and community support, Sakuntala Charitable Trust continues to make a meaningful impact on the lives of countless individuals across the nation.

Our Initiatives

We focus on several key areas to create lasting social impact

Social Service

We engage in various community service activities to address immediate needs and build stronger, more resilient communities.

Human Rights Protection

We work to protect and promote human rights through awareness campaigns, legal aid, and advocacy efforts.

Child & Women Welfare

Our programs focus on the empowerment, safety, and development of children and women from marginalized communities.

Free Education

We provide free education to children above 4 years old, ensuring access to quality learning opportunities.

Support for SC/ST & EWS

We offer specialized support programs for Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, and Economically Weaker Sections.

Health Initiatives

We organize health camps, awareness programs, and provide medical assistance to underserved communities.

Become a Volunteer

Join our mission to create positive change in communities across India

Volunteers are the backbone of our organization. Your time, skills, and passion can make a real difference in the lives of those we serve.

We offer various volunteering opportunities including:

  • Teaching and mentoring children
  • Organizing community events and awareness campaigns
